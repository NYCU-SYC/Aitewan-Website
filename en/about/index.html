About AItewan BioMedical Technology Inc.

Translating medical-imaging AI into clinically usable, reviewable and integrated medical-device software

AItewan translates medical-imaging AI research into clinically usable, reviewable and workflow-integrated medical-device software.

  • NYCU spin-off startup
  • TFDA · FDA 510(k) clearance experience
  • ISO 13485 quality system

AItewan BioMedical Technology Inc. is an AI medical-technology spin-off approved by National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University. It was co-founded by Distinguished Professor Yu-Te Wu and Dr. Wan-Yuo Guo, with technology originating from two National Science and Technology Council research-entrepreneurship programs.

The DeepBT product family focuses on AI-assisted brain tumor analysis, helping clinical teams reduce repetitive contouring workload, improve contour-preparation efficiency, and return AI results as standardized DICOM objects to existing PACS, TPS and clinical imaging workflows for physician review, confirmation or modification.

DeepBT has received the Future Tech Award, the National Innovation Award and the National Innovation Elite Award, and has achieved Taiwan TFDA Class II registration and U.S. FDA 510(k) clearance. Beyond its own products, AItewan provides practical consulting and submission support for medical-AI and SaMD teams across regulatory strategy, quality systems, clinical validation, cybersecurity documentation and medical-device submissions.

Mission and Vision

AItewan applies artificial intelligence to improve care quality, reduce physician workload, and support faster, objective, quantitative and accurate clinical care while advancing medical AI deployment and international expansion.

Core Capabilities

Core capabilities

From imaging AI to clinical integration

Brain Tumor MRI Analysis

Deep-learning MRI analysis and tumor-region detection for brain metastases, meningiomas and acoustic neuromas.

AI-Assisted Contouring

Preliminary tumor contours that physicians can review, confirm or modify, reducing repetitive manual contouring work.

DICOM Clinical Workflow Integration

Standardized DICOM PR / RTSS output that returns AI results to existing PACS, TPS and clinical imaging workflows.

Radiotherapy Workflow Support

Contour preparation for radiotherapy planning while preserving the physician's familiar clinical review environment.

SaMD Productization and Clearance

Hands-on Taiwan TFDA and U.S. FDA 510(k) clearance experience, with practical knowledge of the SaMD development-to-submission path.

Quality Systems and IP Strategy

Experience with ISO 13485 quality systems, medical-device cybersecurity documentation and AI medical-imaging patent portfolios.
